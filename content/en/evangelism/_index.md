Evangelicalism is the historic, biblical faith built on the Gospel — the **Good News** that God saves sinners through the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

---

## Evangelicalism is NOT...

- **A sect** — Evangelicals profess the apostolic faith held by Christians across the centuries
- **A foreign movement** — biblical faith knows no national borders; it is universal
- **Brainwashing** — freedom of conscience and personal conviction are at the heart of the Evangelical faith
- **Coercion or manipulation** — no one is pressured; each person comes to faith freely and by their own will

---

## What is Evangelicalism?

Evangelicalism is a branch of Protestant Christianity faithful to the Holy Trinity — the God of the Bible.

- **"Gospel"** translates from Greek as **"good news"**
- Focus on **Jesus Christ** — the only Savior of the world from sin
- Faith in the **Bible** as the inspired and infallible Word of God
- Salvation **by faith alone** in Jesus Christ — not by works, rituals, or merit
- Every believer has **direct access to God** through prayer, without intermediaries

---

## Five Core Beliefs

Evangelicalism rests on five simple but profound convictions:

- **Scripture alone** — the Bible is the only supreme authority for faith, not tradition or church leadership
- **Faith alone** — salvation comes only through faith in Jesus Christ, not through good works or rituals
- **God's grace alone** — salvation is an undeserved gift from God; we cannot earn it by anything we do
- **Christ alone** — Jesus is the only Savior and Mediator between God and humanity
- **For God's glory alone** — the purpose of life is to glorify God in everything we do

## Evangelicalism and Bulgaria

Evangelicals have deep roots in the history of Bulgaria:

- Evangelical missionaries told the world about the **Batak Massacre**, helping bring about Bulgaria's liberation from Ottoman rule
- The family of **John Atanasoff** — the father of the modern computer — was saved by Evangelicals
- The first Bulgarian to graduate from **Harvard** — Stoyan Vatralski — was an Evangelical and the first preacher of our church in Haskovo
- Evangelicals established some of the **first modern schools** in Bulgaria
- The **first translation of the Bible** into modern Bulgarian was the work of Protestant missionaries, including Petko Slaveykov
